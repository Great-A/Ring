A Long-Running Tradition …
From Biblical Times

From archaeological evidence, we know family coat of arms rings date back to the Old Testament era. During the days of Moses, signet rings were a symbol of family heritage, a means of displaying one’s position, and a tool for applying a personal signature. In Medieval times, knights used their coat of arms to identify themselves – and to keep from being mistaken for enemy soldiers. Your Family Crest Ring links you to ancestors from centuries ago.
